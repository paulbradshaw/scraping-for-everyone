                  Trolley and Ward Figures December 2016

                  -    7,890 Admitted patients on trolleys

                  The Irish Nurses and Midwives Organisation (INMO), in its latest Trolley/Ward Watch analysis, has recorded 7890 patients on trolleys in december 2016 awaiting admission for in-patient treatment. This represents a 29% increase when compared to December 2015.
